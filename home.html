What We Do/Why Choose Us

We provide a concise summary of a patient’s treatment history. Our summaries offer the following advantages:

-All summaries are prepared in a chronological, paragraph format, with treatments and dates in bold font, ensuring that each treatment encounter is easy to locate within the document.

-Each summary is prepared by a Registered Nurse with an extensive background in risk management.

-We do not offer legal or medical advice. Rather, our summaries are non-biased and objective, focusing on the facts related to a patient’s history of treatments.

Whether you are an attorney representing an injured worker, a physician needing to sort through a patient’s treatment history, or a claims administrator who needs to better understand a claimant’s prior treatment history, we hope to work with you. Our services are best suited for stakeholders with large files who need clarity about an individual’s treatments over the life of a claim. Our focused approach to medical record review and summary ensures that the most pertinent information is included.

Our areas of specialization include:

  • Workers’ Compensation
  • Personal Injury
  • Orthopedics
  • Chronic Pain
  • Psychiatry

If the specialty you are looking for is not listed, please email us at info@summarizeit.us

*Most summaries are completed within a few business days. Please allow up to 15 business days for receipt of your summary.
